About the Site and Creator

Hello, I'm Nirmallo, the creator of this website dedicated to the game of Ludo.

I have always been fascinated by the simplicity and excitement that Ludo brings to both children and adults. The strategic decisions and the element of chance make it a timeless classic that can be enjoyed by people of all ages.

As a developer, I wanted to showcase my skills by creating a website that not only provides information about the game but also offers a platform for players to share their experiences and reviews. I hope this website captures the essence of Ludo and brings together fellow enthusiasts.

Thank you for visiting and I hope you enjoy exploring the site!
